Khattraction

The goal is simple : you need to shoot the purple target while avoiding the walls. You can influence the trajectory of the bullets with attractors and repulsors.

Controls :
  • Space to shoot bullets
  • W/S to change the angle of the launcher
  • Drag the attractors/repulsors with left click
  • Remove an attractor/repulsor with right click
  • Change the strenght of an attractor/repulsor by dragging it horizontally with right click
